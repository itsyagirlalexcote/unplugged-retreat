Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

The retreat is located about an hour outside of Montreal, Qc. You will need to travel by car as there is no public transit. Please see the google maps location on the "About" page.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any kind of team! Most of our groups work in office spaces and are searching for a retreat from the desk. We accommodate many groups from companies based in cities.

What size teams can you accommodate?

Group sizes of 5-20 people are generally recommended.We can accommodate up to 20 people per group. If needed a company could split into multiple groups as well.

Is there really no wifi available?

Most sites are indeed wifi-free zones. However, we understand there may be emergencies, so in these cases it is possible to access wifi at the reception desk area.
