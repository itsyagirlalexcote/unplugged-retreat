Our Mission - we want to make you the team you always wanted to be.

Team bonding is an important notion in social psychology that we fully support! We want to bring teams together in order to increase productivity and cohesiveness in the workplace. Team bonding is at the heart of Unplugged Retreat's values.

We believe that the most efficient place to get to know your work peers is in an environment without distractions and technology. Our goal is to quickly and effectively bring people together over the course of a weekend, in a relaxing way in the great outdoors.

Contact us, because you all need this.
